Cost Pathways

How costs are considered across Prism Realty services.

01

Property Management

Management costs are usually shaped by rent, leasing work, inspection cadence and the level of owner reporting or maintenance coordination required.

  • Ongoing management scope
  • Leasing, reletting or renewal work
  • Routine inspections and reporting
  • Maintenance and compliance coordination
02

Property Sales

Selling costs may include Prism's selling fee plus campaign items such as photography, portal advertising, copywriting, styling, auctioneer or preparation work.

  • Sale method and campaign strategy
  • Marketing and advertising selections
  • Presentation, styling and preparation
  • Negotiation and campaign reporting
03

Buyers Agency

Buyers agency costs depend on the search brief, suburb coverage, due diligence required and whether the work involves search, negotiation, auction bidding or full acquisition.

  • Search brief and shortlist depth
  • Inspection and due diligence support
  • Negotiation or auction bidding
  • Settlement pathway coordination
04

SMSF & Finance Coordination

Prism can coordinate property conversations with licensed specialists where SMSF, lending, tax or financial advice is needed. Those professional costs are quoted separately.

  • Property brief alignment
  • Introductions to licensed professionals
  • Clear separation from regulated advice
  • Coordination across next steps

Separate Costs

Some costs sit outside Prism Realty's service fee.

Depending on the service, clients may need to budget for third-party professionals, marketing suppliers, reports, advertising, conveyancing, building and pest inspections, finance, tax or financial advice. Prism identifies these likely items early and can coordinate introductions where specialist support is needed, while keeping those costs and advice separate from Prism's own service fee.

Questions

Useful cost questions to ask early.

Are fees fixed or percentage-based?

It depends on the service and the property brief. Prism confirms the proposed fee structure in writing after understanding the scope.

Are marketing costs included?

Sales campaign costs can vary by property and advertising strategy. Prism separates these items clearly so clients can see what is agency fee and what is campaign spend.

Do I need specialist advice?

SMSF, lending, tax, legal and financial advice must be provided by appropriately licensed professionals. Prism can coordinate introductions through its professional network where needed.

When do I receive the proposal?

After the initial conversation and once the property goal, likely service scope and third-party considerations are clear enough to quote responsibly.
