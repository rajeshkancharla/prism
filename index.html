Services

Core real estate services, supported by careful coordination.

01

Property Management

For landlords who want careful tenant selection, dependable communication and a clear view of how their investment is performing.

  • Rental appraisal and leasing strategy
  • Tenant screening, lease setup and renewals
  • Routine inspections, repairs and maintenance coordination
  • Rent reviews, arrears follow-up and owner reporting
02

Property Sales

A calm, well-run campaign from preparation to negotiation, with presentation and buyer follow-up treated as seriously as the final price.

  • Market appraisal and campaign strategy
  • Styling, photography and listing preparation
  • Open homes, buyer feedback and vendor updates
  • Private treaty and auction negotiation support
03

Buyers Agency

Guided search and negotiation support for families, professionals and investors buying across the Upper North Shore.

  • Search brief, suburb fit and property shortlisting
  • Inspection support and due diligence coordination
  • Price guidance and negotiation strategy
  • Auction bidding and settlement pathway support
+

SMSF & Finance Coordination

Additional property support for clients exploring SMSF purchase structures, lending pathways or investment finance conversations.

  • Property search aligned to client briefs and risk tolerance
  • Coordination with licensed financial, lending and tax specialists
  • Clear separation between property guidance and regulated advice
  • Introductions to suitable professional partners where needed
