Service Options

How we can help!

Option 1

Complete Buyer Representation

A complete buying service for clients who want support from the first brief through to purchase. Prism can help clarify the search criteria, assess suitable suburbs, identify opportunities, review value, coordinate due diligence and manage the negotiation pathway.

  • Search brief and suburb fit
  • Property shortlisting and review
  • Comparable sales and value guidance
  • Due diligence and settlement coordination
  • Leasing Support
Option 2

Offer & Negotiation Support

For clients who have found a property and want help before making a move. Prism can review the property, test the likely value, shape the offer strategy and support communication with the selling agent through the negotiation.

  • Property and price review
  • Offer strategy and negotiation plan
  • Agent communication support
  • Guidance through exchange and next steps
Option 3

Auction Representation

For buyers who have selected an auction property and want calm representation on the day. Prism can help prepare the bidding limit, set the strategy and bid on the client's behalf so the process stays disciplined.

  • Pre-auction preparation
  • Bidding strategy and limit setting
  • On-the-day representation
  • Post-auction next-step support
Option 4

Property Portfolio Review

For owners and investors who already hold property and want a clearer view of what to do next. Prism can review the current position, market evidence, rental performance and the practical pros and cons of holding, improving, leasing or selling.

  • Current property position review
  • Rental and market evidence
  • Hold, sell or improve considerations
  • Practical next-step planning
Option 5

Consultation & Advice

For owners, buyers or investors who want an informed second opinion before making a property decision. Prism can help review the situation, clarify the available pathways, identify practical next steps and provide guidance before clients commit to a campaign, offer or broader strategy.

  • Independent review of the property situation
  • Advice on possible pathways and next steps
  • Preparation before selling, buying or holding
  • Support through key property decisions
Option 6

Investment Property Management

For landlords who want careful ongoing management of their investment property. Prism can support leasing, tenant communication, inspections, repairs, compliance coordination, rent reviews and clear owner reporting.

  • Rental appraisal and leasing support
  • Tenant screening and lease coordination
  • Inspections, maintenance and compliance
  • Owner communication and reporting
