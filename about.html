Grounded in the North Shore

Advice shaped by the place Priyanka and Smitha call home.

Prism Realty is built from a close understanding of the North Shore because its directors are based here. Their perspective is shaped by the everyday details that matter to owners, buyers and investors: school catchments, transport links, family rhythms, rental demand, buyer expectations and the character of each pocket from Hornsby through to Lindfield.

This is not a market they observe from a distance. Priyanka and Smitha live the same local rhythms as the people they advise: the station commute, the school run, the weekend grocery shop, the familiar streets, conversations with neighbours and the quiet details that make one pocket of the North Shore feel different from another.

That lived local context is paired with practical property experience and a relationship-led way of working. Clients are not passed through a generic process. They work with directors who take time to understand the property, the people involved and the decision that needs to be made.
