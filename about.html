Boutique, by design

Your property journey, guided by people who have lived it.

Property isn't just our business, it's our passion. Priyanka and Smitha have spent the last 15 years building their own investment portfolios across Australia, which means the advice you get hasn't come from a textbook. It's come from buying, holding, leasing and selling real properties, making real decisions and learning what actually moves the needle on an investment.

That experience is backed by genuine, in-depth market research. Before we recommend a suburb, a price or a pathway, we've done the work: comparable sales, rental evidence, demand signals and the policy detail that can quietly make or break a decision. We also bring a trusted network of finance brokers, conveyancers, property managers, stylists and other specialists who can support you at every stage of the purchase and sourcing journey.

We're a small agency with two co-founders, and that's deliberate. It means you're never a file number in a system. Your investment journey is personal, and we treat it that way, staying close to every conversation, every decision and every milestone along the way. It's also why we only take on five clients a month, so your property decision always gets our full attention.
